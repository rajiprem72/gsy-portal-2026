Our Vision

Global School of Yoga is committed to promoting Yoga across Tamil Nadu and India through systematic competitions. Beginning with District Level Yoga Competitions, talented participants progress to State Level, National Level and eventually International Level Yoga Competitions. Our mission is to inspire discipline, confidence, physical fitness, mental well-being and excellence among students while preserving the rich heritage of Yogasana.
